Frequently Asked Questions

General

Q: What is UrgentCover?
A: UrgentCover is an online platform that connects dental practices with qualified locum dental professionals (dentists, nurses, hygienists, therapists, receptionists) for temporary staffing needs. It allows you to quickly find and book locums, manage bookings, and handle payments, all in one place.
Q: How is UrgentCover different from a traditional locum agency?
A: Unlike traditional agencies, UrgentCover gives you direct control. You set the hourly rates, you review locum profiles and qualifications, and you choose who to book. There are no hefty agency fees, just a small service fee per booking. We provide the platform and tools; you make the hiring decisions.

Finding & Booking Locums

Q: How do I find locums on UrgentCover?
A: You can search for locums by job type, location, availability, and hourly rate. You can also view locum profiles, which include their qualifications, experience, GDC number, and any reviews from other practices.
Q: What happens if I need to cancel a booking?
A: You can cancel a booking through the platform. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on how much notice is given. Please see our Terms & Conditions for the full cancellation policy.

Data Protection & Security

Q: How is my data protected?
A: We take data protection very seriously and comply with all applicable data protection laws, including UK GDPR. Please see our Privacy Policy for detailed information on how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Support

Q: How do I contact UrgentCover if I have a question?
A: You can contact us through the "Contact Us" form on our website or by email at info@urgentcover.co.uk. Our support team is available from 8am to 6pm, Monday to Friday.
